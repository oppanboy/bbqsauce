Interesting facts about the BBQ sauce:

  • BBQ sauce is used as a flavoring sauce, a marinade, basting, condiment, or topping for meat cooked in the barbecue cooking style, including pork or beef ribs and chicken.
  • Some place the origin of barbecue sauce at the formation of the first American colonies in the 17th century.
  • References to the sauce start occurring in both English and French literature over the next two hundred years.
